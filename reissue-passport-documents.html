Marriage, Divorce and Separated Case

For addition of spouse name in passport:

  • MARRIAGE CERTIFICATE or
  • Joint Photo Declaration signed by both husband and wife (Annexure J)

For deletion of Spouse Name:

  • Divorce Order/Decree

For Change of Spouse Name:

  • Divorce Order/Decree or Death Certificate of first spouse and [any additional supporting document]
